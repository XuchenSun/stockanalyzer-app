概述

股智（StockPulse AI）是一款AI驱动的股票分析iOS应用。我们非常重视您的隐私保护。本隐私政策详细说明了我们如何收集、使用和保护您的信息。

信息收集与使用

我们收集和使用的信息包括：

  • 股票行情数据：通过 Finnhub API 获取公开市场数据，包括股票价格、交易量、历史数据等。这些数据为公开信息，不涉及您的个人信息。
  • AI分析请求：当您使用AI分析功能时，您的查询内容会通过OpenAI API进行处理。我们仅发送股票相关查询，不会发送您的个人身份信息。
  • 自选股列表：您的关注列表存储在您的设备本地，不会上传至我们的服务器。
  • 订阅信息：如果您订阅了高级功能，支付信息由Apple App Store处理，我们不会直接接触您的付款详情。

第三方服务

本应用使用以下第三方服务：

  • Finnhub API：用于获取实时及历史股票数据。数据使用受 Finnhub 使用条款约束。
  • OpenAI API：用于AI股票分析功能。您的查询内容会发送至OpenAI服务器进行处理。请参阅OpenAI的隐私政策了解其数据处理方式。
  • Apple App Store：用于处理应用内购买和订阅。

数据存储与安全

您的个人数据（如自选股列表、设置偏好）均存储在您的iOS设备本地。所有API通信均使用HTTPS加密传输。我们不运营用户数据服务器，不收集或存储您的个人投资组合信息。

订阅服务

本应用提供订阅制高级功能。订阅通过Apple App Store管理，包括：

  • 订阅费用通过您的Apple ID账户收取
  • 订阅将自动续费，除非在当前周期结束前至少24小时取消
  • 您可在iOS设置 > Apple ID > 订阅中管理或取消订阅

用户权利

您有权：

  • 随时删除应用及其本地数据
  • 管理或取消订阅服务
  • 联系我们了解您的数据使用情况

政策更新

Overview

StockPulse AI (股智) is an AI-powered stock analysis iOS application. We take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information Collection and Use

We collect and use the following information:

  • Stock Market Data: We retrieve publicly available market data through the Finnhub API, including stock prices, volume, and historical data. This is public information and does not involve your personal data.
  • AI Analysis Requests: When you use the AI analysis feature, your queries are processed through the OpenAI API. We only send stock-related queries and never transmit your personal identity information.
  • Watchlist: Your watchlist is stored locally on your device and is never uploaded to our servers.
  • Subscription Information: If you subscribe to premium features, payment processing is handled entirely by the Apple App Store. We never directly access your payment details.

Third-Party Services

This app uses the following third-party services:

  • Finnhub API: For retrieving real-time and historical stock data. Usage is subject to Finnhub's terms of service.
  • OpenAI API: For AI-powered stock analysis features. Your queries are sent to OpenAI servers for processing. Please refer to OpenAI's Privacy Policy for their data handling practices.
  • Apple App Store: For processing in-app purchases and subscriptions.

Data Storage and Security

Your personal data (such as watchlists and preferences) is stored locally on your iOS device. All API communications are encrypted using HTTPS. We do not operate user data servers and do not collect or store your personal investment portfolio information.

Subscription Services

This app offers premium features through subscriptions managed via the Apple App Store:

  • Subscription fees are charged to your Apple ID account
  • Subscriptions auto-renew unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period
  • You can manage or cancel subscriptions in iOS Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete the app and all its local data at any time
  • Manage or cancel subscription services
  • Contact us to inquire about your data usage

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated policies will be posted on this page with a revised date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
